Network Solutions For Tomorrow's Demands

STRUCTURED CABLING

The logical and efficient use of a complete system of cabling and associated hardware, Structured Cabling creates a comprehensive network infrastructure to facilitate data and voice communication. Get reliable connectivity of computer networks, land phone lines and VoIP systems with precision low voltage copper network cabling and extend the range of wireless networks with additional wireless access points (WAPS). C-Comm offers the most advanced structured cabling systems available from industry leaders like Belden and CommScope. A professionally installed structured cabling system makes future moves, adds and changes (MACs) easy.

FIBER OPTIC CABLING

Capable of higher bandwidth and data rates over greater distances, fiber optic backbones are often used in larger networks connecting multiple LANs or subnets. C-Comm's fiber optic services include termination of multi-mode and single-mode fiber. C-Comm utilizes the latest advanced fusion splicing equipment to ensure spliced cables deliver optimum performance and reliability. All C-Comm fiber network installations are fully tested and certified using Fluke CertiFiber® Pro and/or OptiFiber® Pro OTDR.

CORPERATE AUDIO/VIDEO & HOME THEATER

Comprehensive audio-visual systems have become an important tool for many businesses. Let C-Comm improve your company’s sales, training and presentation capabilities with a complete A/V system installation. C-Comm also installs residential home theater systems.
